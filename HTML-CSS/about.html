    About Us

Who are we?

The company Palindrom with its headquarters in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina is registered as an association for tourism, trade, and services. We have been present on the touristic market since 2010.

During the whole year we take care about our current, but also future clients, and also offer various services like hotel booking to satisfy everyone's needs. The main motto of our agency is to allow you to book your desired destination on time. Clients' desires and requirements are at the first place. The members of our team are young individiuals who are able to identify themselves with the company's goals and industriously work to accomplish those goals.
